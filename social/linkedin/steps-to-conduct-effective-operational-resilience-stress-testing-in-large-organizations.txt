In today’s complex risk landscape, operational resilience stress testing is essential for large organizations to proactively identify weaknesses and ensure business continuity under pressure. Effectively designed stress tests provide actionable insights to strengthen defenses and navigate disruptions with confidence.

Key steps to conduct impactful stress testing include:
• Defining clear objectives aligned with organizational risks  
• Simulating realistic adverse scenarios  
• Engaging cross-functional stakeholders for comprehensive insights  
• Analyzing results to identify critical vulnerabilities  
• Integrating findings into continuous improvement plans
