In today’s complex risk landscape, operational resilience is no longer optional—it’s a strategic imperative. Embedding resilience into corporate governance structures ensures that organizations can better anticipate, respond to, and recover from disruptions, safeguarding both operational continuity and stakeholder trust. Integrating these principles requires a thoughtful alignment of risk management processes with governance oversight.

Key considerations include:  
- Aligning operational resilience with strategic decision-making  
- Enhancing board engagement and accountability  
- Integrating risk frameworks with business continuity planning  
- Leveraging data and technology for proactive risk identification  
- Fostering a culture of resilience across all organizational levels
